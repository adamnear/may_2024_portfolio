About this project

This project is designed to enhance the buyer's experience at a mock record store. It provides a seamless interface for browsing, searching, and purchasing records while emphasizing an intuitive and enjoyable shopping experience.

Personal Reflection

This project allowed me to deepen my understanding of CRUD operations and strengthen my skills in developing user-friendly interfaces. It also reinforced the importance of aligning technical solutions with user needs to create an impactful experience.

Next steps for this project

As this was a school assignment, my next steps will be to expand my knowledge in areas such as advanced front-end development and responsive design to further enhance the user experience in future projects. I also plan to explore integrating payment gateways and more complex database structures for e-commerce applications.
