About this project

This project was an idea of mine that came to life through a semester long capstone project. The goal was to assist HRM residents in being aware of street cleaning schedules in their area, as a way to save the time and be able to park with confidence of not resieving a ticket.

Personal reflection

As project manager of this application, I oversaw all parts of the development and had taken care of any prep work, research and presenting of materials. This was a great exercise in managing a project andensuring clear communication between team members, meeting deadlines, and delivering a cohesive and functional application. It allowed me to refine my leadership skills and develop a deeper understanding of the development lifecycle from concept to completion.

Next steps for this project

Myself and the team will continue to work on this project to allow for user account creations, which in turn will let users upload the parking restrictions on their street. The goal for this application is for it to be an open sourced platform, so the database can grow to cover many streets in the HRM.
